Customer Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13/02/2026

1. Introduction

Countryside Safari Agency respects the privacy of its customers and is committed to protecting personal information collected through our website, booking platforms, phone, email, and messaging services such as WhatsApp. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information necessary to provide travel and tourism services. This includes:

  • Personal identification: Full name, phone number, email address, passport details.
  • Travel info: Flight details, hotel reservations, itineraries, car rentals.
  • Payment info: Billing details, payment confirmations.
  • Technical info: Website usage data, device information, cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for legitimate business purposes, including processing bookings, coordinating with airlines/hotels, communicating updates, providing emergency assistance, and complying with regulations. Countryside Safari Agency does not sell or rent customer personal data to third parties.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share necessary information with trusted partners only when required to deliver travel services (e.g., Airlines, Hotels, Tour Guides, Payment Processors).

5. Data Security & Retention

We implement secure booking platforms and restricted access to protect your personal information. We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal requirements.

6. Your Rights & Contact

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data. For questions, contact:
